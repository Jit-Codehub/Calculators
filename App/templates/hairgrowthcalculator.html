How fast does hair grow? The formula behind hair growth calculator

The average hair growth rate is 0.5 inches per month, so growth in inches per month can be calculated according to the following equation:   L = 0.5 * t, where L represents the length in inches, and t represents the amount of time in months. For the average hair growth rate in centimeters or millimeters per month, 0.5 can be converted and substituted into the equation.
