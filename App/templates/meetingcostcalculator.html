How much does it cost to have a meeting?

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, unnecessary meetings cost U.S. businesses approximately $37 billion each year. That's because meetings are possibly one of the most expensive overhead costs in an organization and often managed inefficiently.

How long should a meeting be?

The average length of a meeting is between 31 to 60 minutes, according to statistics. In the last 15 years, the average duration of a meeting has increased by 10%. In the US, companies hold about 11 million meetings a day in total, which equals 55 million meetings per week and 220 million meetings per year.
