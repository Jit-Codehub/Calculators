The unit of the concentration which measures the number of moles of a solute per litre of solution is known as Molarity. Molar concentration is also known as molarity which can be calculated based on the simple formula, Concentration (mg/ml) / Molecular Weight. Enter the concentration in the above mg/ml to molarity calculator to find the molar concentration or molarity of the solution.

Example:

Calculate molar concentration of the solution having concentration and molecular weight of 5 mg/ml and 7 mg/mmole.

Solution:

Molarity = Concentration / Molecular Weight

= 5 / 7

= 0.7143 mmole/ml
